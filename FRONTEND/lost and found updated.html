Lost & Found Guidelines

Reporting Lost Items

If you've lost an item, please provide as much detail as possible including photos (if available), where you believe you lost it, and any identifying features.

Reporting Found Items

If you've found an item, please submit basic details without revealing unique identifying features that only the owner would know.

Returning Items

Always arrange to meet in public campus locations for item exchanges. Consider meeting at the campus security office for valuable items.

Security Office

The university security office also maintains a physical lost and found. Check with them at the Main Admin Block, or call ext. 1234.
